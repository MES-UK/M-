1.1 Numbers

Mathematics begins with numbers. At first, numbers are used to count and measure. Later, they become objects of study in their own right.

1.2 Equality

The symbol $=$ does not mean “calculate”. It means that two expressions represent the same number.

Example

The expressions $2 + 3$ and $5$ are equal because they represent the same value.

Remark

In advanced mathematics, equality becomes a relation between abstract objects, not just numbers.
